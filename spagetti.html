Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with bacon and eggs

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 6 oz bacon, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 egg yolks

    Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, fry the bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet with a slotted spoon and set aside on a paper towel to drain.
  3. Add the garlic to the bacon fat in the skillet and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, parmesan cheese, and heavy cream.
  5. When the spaghetti is done, drain it and add it to the skillet with the garlic. Toss the spaghetti with the garlic until well-coated.
  6. Add the egg mixture to the skillet and toss everything together until the eggs are cooked and the sauce is creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Divide the spaghetti into bowls and top with the crispy bacon.
