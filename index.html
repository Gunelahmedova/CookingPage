Extremely Delicious Pudding Cotton Cake

Ingredients for Extremely Delicious Pudding Cotton Cake Recipe

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 1 packet of baking soda
  • 1 packet of vanilla
  • 1 packet of chocolate pudding (powdered)

    For above;
  • Icing sugar

How to Make an Extremely Delicious Pudding Cotton Cake Recipe?

  1. First, grease the cake mold with butter or margarine and flour it and reserve it in the refrigerator.
  2. Beat the eggs and sugar well.
  3. Add oil and milk to it and stir a little.
  4. Sift and add the flour, baking powder and vanilla, add the pudding powder to it and beat it.
  5. Take the cake mold out of the fridge and pour the cake mortar into it and bake in a 180 degree oven for 35 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and when the initial temperature is removed, put the serving plate on it and turn it over.
  7. Once the cake has cooled, sprinkle with icing sugar, slice it and serve.
